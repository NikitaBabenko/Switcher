VibeNest Switcher — settings

Privacy

By default, decryption runs entirely on your machine — no text leaves the browser. Bundled languages: 9 (English, Russian, Ukrainian, Belarusian, German, French, Greek, Hebrew, Turkish).

Used only when the checkbox is on. Leave empty to stay fully offline.

Languages

Pick the languages the layout should switch between. At least 2.

Behaviour

Off — extension only decrypts text you have explicitly selected; on supported sites with empty selection it shows a notice instead of touching the field.

When you type a space or punctuation after a word that's clearly in the wrong layout, the extension fixes it in place. Skipped on password fields and on sites excluded by your site policy. Press Backspace to undo.

Site policy

Hostnames match exactly or by suffix (e.g. vk.com also matches m.vk.com). Leave empty for the default (everywhere) mode.

The keyboard shortcut is configured at chrome://extensions/shortcuts.
